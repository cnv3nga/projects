Name: Yu xiang rou si
Categories: Pork, Chinese

  500 g Pork            10 g sugar
   10 g chilly       1 1/2 g salt   
   15 g oil

In large bowl, blend oil and sugars on low until well mixed. Add chilly.
Beat in salt...
